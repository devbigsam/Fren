Welcome to Fren! Grab a cup of coffee and soak this all in! So $fren actually pre dates $wif by several years. Projects that fall into this category - ai slop, tiktok meta, viral zoo animals! These memes as we all know are short lived and never last long given they are news or viral based. This overall isn't good for the space and memes and the only answer is a hard reset back to the OG memes such Wif, Billy, Michi, Shark Cat where meme cults formed, bag working thrived. $Fren could well be the answer to this as these memes tend to have far more longevity and actually bring back that tradition of good memes and bag working mentality. Fren is also an iconic crypto slang term we all use on a daily basis, even the likes of Ryoshi used this in his medium article and the introduction to Shiba Inu! To add Fren is a play on the word Friend just like Wif was a play on the word With, it's extremely normie friendly, it has infinite memeability and a golden 4 letter ticker. This isn't just a random meme we have on our hands..Fren is the answer to reset the game back to how it was.. Join us on this journey Fren...
